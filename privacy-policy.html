Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Camm Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Apps.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Our Apps store your content (such as journal entries, affirmations, vision board images, and preferences) locally on your device. We do not transmit this content to our servers or any third party.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, anonymous usage data to improve our Apps:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous, aggregated data about feature usage (e.g., which screens are visited most)
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs

This data is collected through Apple's built-in analytics and does not personally identify you.

Subscription Information

All subscription purchases and billing are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive anonymous transaction confirmations from RevenueCat, our subscription management service, to verify your subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Apps
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To verify subscription status
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal content is stored only on your device and is not backed up to our servers. If you delete the App, your locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

If you use iCloud backup on your device, your app data may be included in your device backup, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payment processing (Apple Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat receives anonymous transaction data to verify subscription status (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)

5. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Access your data directly within the App
  • Delete your data by deleting the App from your device
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of our Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@cammstudios.com
