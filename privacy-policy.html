Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Camm Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Apps.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Our Apps store your content (such as journal entries, affirmations, vision board images, and preferences) locally on your device. We do not transmit this content to our servers.

During onboarding, some of our Apps may ask you for profile information such as your first name, age range, gender, and personal interests. This information is used to personalise your experience and may be shared with our third-party analytics and paywall services (see Section 4 below).

Automatically Collected Information

We collect usage data to improve our Apps through third-party analytics services:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage analytics: Feature usage events (e.g. screens viewed, actions taken), collected via PostHog
  • App lifecycle events: App launches, sessions, and background transitions
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs

This data is associated with anonymous identifiers and does not include your name, email, or locally stored content.

Subscription Information

All subscription purchases and billing are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription status is managed through Superwall, which receives an anonymous user identifier and purchase receipts to verify your subscription.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Apps
  • To personalise your experience (e.g. tailored paywall offers)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs through analytics
  • To verify subscription status
  • To understand how features are used so we can improve them

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal content (journal entries, affirmations, vision board items, etc.) is stored only on your device and is not backed up to our servers. If you delete the App, your locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

Profile attributes collected during onboarding and usage analytics are transmitted to our third-party service providers (see Section 4). We rely on these providers' security practices to protect this data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Apps use the following third-party services that may receive data:

  • PostHog (analytics) — Receives anonymous usage events, device information, and user attributes. Data is processed on servers in the United States. PostHog Privacy Policy
  • Superwall (paywall and subscription management) — Receives anonymous user identifiers, user profile attributes, and subscription status for paywall personalisation. Superwall Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — Processes all subscription purchases and billing
  • Vercel — Hosts our website and collects anonymous web analytics

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Retention

Locally stored data persists on your device until you delete the App. Analytics data held by third-party providers is retained according to their respective data retention policies. We do not maintain our own servers or databases containing your personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 18, please do not use our Apps. If we learn that we have collected information from a person under 18, we will take steps to delete that information.

7. Your Rights

All Users

Since your content is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all locally stored data by deleting the App.

European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (improving our Apps) and consent (profile data provided during onboarding). To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

Australian Users (Privacy Act 1988)

If you are located in Australia, you have the right to access and correct personal information we hold about you under the Australian Privacy Principles. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our Apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
