Frequently Asked Questions

Is Manifest free to use?

Manifest offers a 3-day free trial so you can explore all features. After the trial, a subscription is required to continue using the app.

What is the 369 method?

The 369 manifesting method involves writing your intention 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times in the evening. This repetition helps focus your energy and align your thoughts with your goals.

Is my journal data private?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We never see or access your personal content.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Find Manifest and tap Cancel. You can also manage subscriptions here.
