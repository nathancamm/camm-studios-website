Privacy Policy — Manifest

Last updated: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the Manifest - Daily Affirmations app ("Manifest") developed by Camm Studios. This supplements our general Privacy Policy.

1. What Data Manifest Stores

All of the following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite affirmations
  • Journal entries (369 method)
  • Vision board images and text goals
  • Daily check-in and streak data
  • Notification preferences and timing
  • Onboarding completion status

We do not have access to any of this data. It never leaves your device.

2. Photos and Camera

Manifest allows you to add photos to your vision board using the iOS Photo Picker. We only access photos you explicitly select — we do not access your full photo library. Selected photos are stored locally within the app's data container.

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, Manifest schedules local notifications on your device. These are processed entirely by iOS and do not involve any external servers.

4. Subscription Data

Your subscription is managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We receive anonymous confirmation of your subscription status (active/expired) but do not receive your name, email, or payment details.

  • RevenueCat receives: anonymous app user ID, purchase receipt, subscription status
  • RevenueCat does not receive: your name, email, journal entries, photos, or any personal content

5. Analytics

Manifest uses Apple's built-in App Analytics only. This provides aggregated, anonymous data such as app sessions, device types, and crash reports. No personal data is collected.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all your Manifest data, simply delete the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, this permanently removes everything.

7. Contact

Questions about Manifest's privacy practices? Email us at support@cammstudios.com.
